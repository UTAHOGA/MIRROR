Design Rules

What This Layout Fixes

One primary path

Enter hunt. Enter points. Read the answer.

Result first

The hunter sees Draw Reality before tables, proof, or explanation blocks.

Proof stays nearby

Draw Pressure and Formula remain visible, but they do not overpower the result.

This is a clean design direction, not a finished engine. It gives you the right structure: Draw Pressure as the proof layer, Draw Reality as the answer layer, and the hunter’s goal close to the inputs so the recommendation can actually mean something.
