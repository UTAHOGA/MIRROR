Result-first research

Bring your saved hunt in, set residency and points, and get a real answer fast.

This page is built to answer the draw question first. We keep the method honest, keep the proof nearby, and keep the Hunt Pack close so a hunter can move through saved hunts without rebuilding the search all over again.

Why this is different

Utah does not use one simple draw formula, so this page does not pretend otherwise. Bonus draws, preference draws, and no-draw hunts stay in their own lanes, and the result is built from the actual hunt selected, not a generic shortcut.

What the engine does
  • Starts with the same hunt's accepted 2025 draw results.
  • Removes the hunters who already drew from that applicant pool.
  • Rolls the remaining hunters forward one point where the draw family requires it.
  • Applies current permit numbers and runs the hunt through the correct Utah draw method.
Why that matters
  • You are not just seeing generic chances at your point level.
  • You are seeing the current hunt re-read against the hunters most likely to be competing directly with you for that same permit.
Guaranteed line explained

Minimum Bonus Points for 100% Initial Draw means the lowest point line that fully clears the max-point allocation before the random side begins.

The Science

Text works better here than the full graphic. It keeps the logic visible without crowding the page.

  • Top-point tags are roughly half the total tags in Utah bonus draws.
  • If the guaranteed point line keeps rising by a full point or more, hunters below that line are losing ground.
  • If new max-point hunters arrive as fast as top-point tags are handed out, point creep becomes permanent.
  • If more top-point tags clear out than new max-point hunters arrive, catch-up can still happen.

What the page does

We factor in last year's successful permittees and remove them from the hunt application pool. Then we normalize current permit numbers and the current max-point structure for the same hunt before we run the selected point level against the updated field.

Why this is more useful

You do not just get a generic answer for “X points.” You get a current-year read built from last year's actual hunt results and today's permit structure, which gives a better picture of the hunters most likely to be competing directly with you.
