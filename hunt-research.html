Quick definitions for prediction and trend.

Draw Prediction

  • Red: This hunt is currently a poor draw setup at your point level.
Point Creep Trend
  • Green: You are catching up.
  • Yellow: The line is mostly holding pace with you.
  • Red: Point creep is outrunning your annual gain.
